Arr Gems: Where melodies sparkle and rhythm shines. Discover the transcendent beauty of AR Rahman's finest compositions, each a gem in its own right. From soul-stirring ballads to pulsating beats, immerse yourself in the brilliance of Rahman's musical mastery. Unearth the treasures of his repertoire and experience the magic of his timeless creations.

WHY AR RAHMAN

AR Rahman has been gifted with a keen sense of production of sound & melody. His knowledge of both Western & Indian styles of music gives him an edge over others. His musical genius has been honed under the tutelage of great Ilaiyaraja who was a musical genius himself.
